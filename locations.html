Bloomington Housing Authority

The Bloomington Housing Authority is located at Woodhill Towers in the building connecting the two towers. Please use the doors on the east side of the building. You may need to ring the doorbell to have someone let you inside.

104 E. Wood Street
Bloomington, IL 61701

Heartland Community College

Our location at Heartland Community College is in the Workforce Development Center building. When entering the building please take the lobby elevator or stairs to the third floor. When exiting the elevator or stairs turn left and follow the VITA signs to our location.

1500 W. Raab Road
Normal, IL 61761

Normal Activity & Recreation Center
